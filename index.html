Teaching
 Rather Than 
Coding

Using traditional quant platforms, implementing even a simple trading algorithm is time-consuming and error-prone. In AgileQuant, AI learns your trading strategy by looking at just a dozen of your trades in a fully simulated environment.

Teaching an AI to trade takes minutes while coding an algorithm to trade takes hours. We believe that your precious time should be spent on thinking genius trading ideas, not wasted on tedious coding work.

Quantify Imprecise 
Trading Ideas

Most trend trading strategies are difficult to quantify because they are intrinsically imprecise. It is a headache to precisely define subjective concepts such as Resistance, Support, Volume Shrinkage and many others.

AgileQuant uses specialized machine learning models with thousands of pre-defined features to turn your imprecise ideas into precise quant trading algorithms. All you need to convey is when to buy and sell.

Iterative Tuning 
Until Ready

Iterative tuning is particularly useful in changing markets where trading strategies have to be continuously optimized. In AgileQuant, you can easily optimize your trading AI at any time.

Once a trading AI is created, it can be reviewed and backtested immediately. You can always teach it again by feeding more trading points or correcting existing ones until you feel it is ready for real trading.
